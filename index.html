Introducing Specs

Specs is a permaweb app dedicated to creating open standards and specifications for building on Arweave. Specs is open to contributions from everyone, and is designed to foster collaboration and coordination amongst builders. Individuals can change and improve existing specifications and help create new standards!

Specs integrates with STAMPs, allowing anyone to vote for their favorite specifications. Specifications with the most STAMPs rise to the top, signaling adoption by the community.
